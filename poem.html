JNGRC'S COMPOSITION

A VALIDATION

In shadows cast by doubt and fear,
A child waits, her future unclear.
Longing for a validation,
For her worth, her place.
Each test a battle, hard-fought ground,
Yet happiness never truly found.

Seeking solace in a letter,
Hoping it will make her better.
But grades alone cannot define,
The brilliance in her heart's design.
She's more than numbers on a page,
Her potential, a boundless stage.

So let her know, in gentle voice,
Her worth transcends a simple choice.
Encourage her to brightly shine,
And watch her soar, her spirit fine.

FORBIDDEN, THEY SAY!

In a hidden place, beneath moonlit skies,
Two hearts entwine, in each other's eyes.
Love knows no bounds, in their shared embrace,
Finding solace and peace in this forbidden space.

Hand in hand, they defy the norm,
Their love blossoms, pure and warm.
Together they stand, unapologetically strong,
In a world where love can never be wrong.

Butterfly

A garden bright, a vision takes flight,
Graceful dancer in the morning light.
Petals mimic its rainbow hues,
A fleeting glimpse of nature's muse.

Silent whispers on zephyrs' wings,
A delicate secret nature brings.
In whispered tales of day untold,
Beauty's dance in colors bold.

DELIGHT IN ONE NIGHT

Beneath the stars, a young girl wanders free,
Under the moon's glow, where shadows be.
Her heart is light, her spirit wild and bright,
Dancing in the dark, lost in the night.

Adventure calls, she answers with glee,
In the shadows, she finds what she seeks to see.
Just for one night, she lets herself sway,
In freedom's embrace, she finds her own way.

Guided by stars, with the wind as her song,
Living in the moment, where she belongs.
A memory of a wild night, shining so clear,
In the soft moonlight, she has nothing to fear.

FAITH

Love's arrival, a surprise so dear,
Heart aflutter, thoughts tangled, crystal clear,
Longing in gazes, treasures to find,
Daydreams dance, shared space in mind.

Connection deep, words cannot convey,
Thrilling journey, hearts in love's sway.
Embrace the journey, hand in hand,
In love's embrace, we'll forever stand.

You had my heart.
You had my soul.
You are Faith.
